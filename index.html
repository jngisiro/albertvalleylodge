Brief

Albertine Valley Lodge, located at the northern part of Queen Elizabeth National park overlooking the Maramagambo Forest and Lake Nyamasingiri, Uganda's largest volcanic crater lake. With 5 rooms all uniquely set up to give you a comfortable stay.


- Single Room -

Our double rooms have one queen sized bed that enhances your comfort and stay. The rooms have beautiful views of the national park and the Maramagambo Forest with clean bathrooms fitted with a shower and bath tabs.
