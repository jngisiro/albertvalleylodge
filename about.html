About Us

About Albert Valley Lodge
All you need to know

Albertine Valley Lodge, located at the northern part of Queen Elizabeth National park overlooking the Maramagambo Forest and Lake Nyamasingiri, Uganda's largest volcanic crater lake. With 5 rooms all uniquely set up to give you a comfortable stay. 7km from Kyambura, with more than 3 routes connecting to the lodge, you can either choose to come through the community route giving you a thrilling adventure as you make your way through different coffee farms and banana plantations with beautiful scenic views of beautiful landscapes or take an adventurous bushy road with baboons an elephants keeping you expectant to get an encounter with the Savannah elephants. Any route you choose expect a great adventure.

Our staff is well trained to take care of you and give you a good stay while with us. Expect an ending smile. Albertine Valley Lodge has done the most beautiful job putting the taste of paradise together in harmony with its surroundings.

Communtiy Support

Albertine Valley Lodge is located in Rubiziri District in Rumuri village at the edge of Queen Elizabeth National Park. Surrounded with Coffee farms, while with us you can take a community walk where you can explore the African villages. Walk through the coffee farms and banana plantation and also get to learn how to make bricks that we use for Building.

Our Neighbours

Working hand in hand with Keep Me In School, a charity organization based in Uganda, at Albertine Valley Lodge we aim at improving the well being of the people around us. Through this Organization we have managed to extend a hand to different school going children most especially at Buhingo 3 primary School, our neighboring school through giving them scholastic materials and mentorship. So for every ten dollars you pay 1% is directed to supporting the community around us.
